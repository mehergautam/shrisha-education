How to Join

Admission Process

Simple 4-step process to start your digital journey with Shrisha Education.

  • 1
    Fill the Enquiry Form
    Submit your details and preferred course online or visit us in person at Bhatkuli center.
  • 2
    Document Verification
    Bring Aadhaar card, 10th mark sheet, 2 passport photos and any other relevant documents.
  • 3
    Fee Payment
    Pay via Cash, UPI, Net Banking or Cheque. Receipt and ERA login provided instantly.
  • 4
    Start Learning
    Get your ERA portal access and begin attending classes — both lab and online sessions available.
