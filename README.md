## 🚨 Why This Website Exists

Students in Bhatkuli and surrounding Amravati district lacked a **single trustworthy digital presence** for their nearest government-recognized IT education center. Before this website:

- 📵 No online presence — students relied solely on word-of-mouth
- 📋 No way to enquire or apply for courses online
- ❓ No clarity on which MKCL courses are offered locally
- 📍 No map or directions for students from nearby villages

**This website solves all of that — and looks world-class doing it.**

## 🤝 About the Center

**Shrisha Education** is an officially authorized **MKCL (Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited)** learning center located in **Bhatkuli, Amravati, Maharashtra**.

- 🏛️ Authorized to deliver all official MKCL & KLiC courses
- 🎓 Certificates recognized by **YCMOU** (Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University)
- 🌐 Connected to **ERA eLearning platform** for hybrid learning
- 💼 Offers free **placement assistance** for deserving students
- 📅 Operating since **2013** — 10+ years of excellence
